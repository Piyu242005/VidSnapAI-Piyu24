## Color Usage Guide

### **Mint Green (#c5f9d7)**
- Success icons
- Subtle accents
- Fresh, positive feedback

### **Golden Yellow (#f7d486)**
- Borders and outlines
- Secondary buttons
- Stats badges
- Icons
- Hover states

### **Coral Red (#f27a7d)**
- Primary buttons
- Headings and titles
- Download buttons
- Active states
- Important CTAs

### **White (rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.9-0.95))**
- Card backgrounds
- Content containers
- Modal backgrounds
- Creates clean, modern look over gradient

### **Dark Text (#2c3e50, #34495e, #555)**
- Main text content
- Headings
- Labels
- Ensures readability on light backgrounds
