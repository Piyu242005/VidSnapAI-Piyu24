Drag & Drop Image Upload

Easily upload multiple images by dragging and dropping them into the upload zone. No need to click through file dialogs - just drag your images and start creating!

  • Visual feedback during upload

See all your uploaded images in a beautiful preview grid. Drag and drop to reorder them, or use the up/down buttons to arrange them exactly how you want.

  • Thumbnail preview for each image

Set how long each image appears in your reel. Control the timing from 0.5 seconds to 5 seconds per image for perfect pacing.

  • Fine-tune your reel's rhythm

Automatic Reel Generation

  • Fast processing times

Progress Tracking
